Class Levels

Classes grouped by playing strength.

We run weekly classes for every level, from brand new players to tournament competitors. Pick a level to see what your child will work on. Not sure where they fit? Call us and we will help.

Brand new players start here. We make the board feel friendly fast and build real confidence game by game.

FocusWhat it builds
How the pieces moveA solid foundation with no gaps.
Basic checkmatesThe confidence to finish a game.
Simple tacticsSpotting forks, pins, and easy wins.
Good habitsThinking before moving, every time.

For players who know the rules and want to win more games. We sharpen pattern recognition and real game skill.

FocusWhat it builds
Opening principlesStrong starts without memorizing lines.
Tactical patternsSeeing combinations two moves ahead.
Endgame basicsConverting a lead into a win.
Game reviewLearning from every game played.

For competitive players sharpening for rated play. We build the depth that wins tournaments.

FocusWhat it builds
Deep opening prepA repertoire your child owns.
Complex tacticsCalculating sharp positions accurately.
Endgame techniquePrecision when it matters most.
Tournament strategyManaging clock, nerves, and pressure.

Classes run weekly and are held live online over Zoom. Reach out to confirm openings and to get your child placed in the right group and time.

Why Families Stay

Big perks, included free.

No paid trial classes here. Every enrolled student gets these at no extra cost.

🏆

Free Monthly Tournaments

Every student can play in free USCF rated tournaments each month, held in person and online. Rated events normally cost money, so this is a real saving and a great way to grow.

🤝

Free Extra Help

If a student falls behind or needs help with homework, they get an extra class at no charge. We make sure no child is left behind.

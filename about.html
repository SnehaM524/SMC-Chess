Our Story

A premier home for chess learning.

SMC Chess Club was founded in 2020 on the principles of discipline, logic, and inclusivity. We have quickly become a premier destination for chess excellence.

We do not just teach moves. We teach students how to think critically under pressure. Our coaching staff brings 15 years of experience to every session.

We blend expert coaching with a fun and welcoming vibe. From beginners to advanced players, our personalized training sharpens minds and builds confidence.

We also offer free classes for differently abled children. Everyone deserves a seat at the board.
